About

Welcome to Marsalforn Mariners FC!

Based in the heart of Marsalforn, Gozo, we’re more than a football club we’re a community. Our mission is simple: grow the game, develop local talent, and bring people together through football. We compete in the Gozo Football Youth League and run a youth academy for U16 and U19 teams.



Youth Academy:

Our academy is all about nurturing young players and preparing them for the big stage. Standout performers in our U16 and U19 squads have the chance to progress to the First Team, giving homegrown talent a real pathway to senior football. We’re proud that our First Team reflects our local roots while helping every player reach their full potential on and off the pitch.



Join the Mariners Family:

Whether you’re a young player chasing your football dreams or a fan passionate about local sport, Marsalforn Mariners FC is the place to be. Together, we grow talent, celebrate the game, and strengthen our community.
